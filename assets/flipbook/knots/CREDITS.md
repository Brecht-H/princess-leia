## Notes

- **Bowline, Reef knot, Sheet bend, Double sheet bend, Figure-eight, Rolling hitch**
  are from a single consistent black-line diagram family by Wikimedia user
  **Lucasbosch** — the diagrams used on the English Wikipedia knot articles.
  Rendered from the source SVG at 900px and flattened onto a white background.
- **Mastworp (clove hitch)** is the Dutch-Wikipedia public-domain coloured diagram
  by **Sawims** (orange working line over a green spar).
- **Kikker (cleat hitch)** is a public-domain photograph showing the completed
  figure-of-eight + locking hitch on a cleat, with a yellow arrow indicating the
  finishing direction (author Schorschi2). Chosen because no clean CC vector
  step-diagram of a cleat hitch exists on Commons; this photo is unambiguous.
- **Rondtorn** is the CC BY-SA grayscale line diagram of a round turn and two half
  hitches around a bar/rail.
